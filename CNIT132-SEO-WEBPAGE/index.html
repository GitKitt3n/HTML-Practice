SEO Best Practices

Let's start off with what SEO stands for. I was never good with acronyms but this is one I come across far too often not to remember. SEO stands for search engine optimization. From my reading and years of work in eCommerce I know that SEO is the practice of bettering not only site visibility but how viewers reach your site.

A bit about Search Engines

Search engines are systems that find the information that's out there on the web. Search engines can help how often your content is found on the web. Eventhough Google is not the only By practicing good SEO practice you search tool out there, it does make up about 92% of all search volumn according to Kelly Main, writer of 11 Essential Google search Statistics for 2021.

Technical SEO

Think of search engine optimization as the methods and practices that really build your online presence. With SEO the most important types are On-Page, Off-Page, and Technical SEO. For Technical SEO you want to make sure that you optimize your URL structure, you fix any crawling errors, and you have SEO friendly design principles. That means that your site is accessible, you have a good internal link structure, and you're thinking about UX.

On-Page SEO

With on page SEO your want to look at how you optimize your page titles because they are what's shown in search results. You want your titles to be engaging so viewers make those clicks. You also want to make sure your titles have enough information about the actual page content. For your images, make sure you have optimized file names and use ALT tags! That is a big MUST for images. If for some reason your image shows up as broken you have that alt text to describe the image that is supposed to be visible.

Off-Page SEO

Off page SEO deals more with ways in which you promote your site. This means brand promotions, getting backlinks from other sites, and social media engagement.
